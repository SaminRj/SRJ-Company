About Us:

SRJ company started its activity in the field of women's clothing in 2024. The name of this company is derived from the name of its founder, Samin Raja. From 2028, it expanded its activity in the field of women's clothing and started producing men's clothing. Today, this company is one of the most well-known brands in the field of clothing.

The products of this brand are available in 3 ways:

  1. buy from the online shop and send to all places
  2. Show the desired model and place your order
  3. Present your overall style and leave the clothing design to us

How to order the product?

Ordering clothes in this collection is done in 2 ways:
- You can share the photo of the dress you want in the special form of this section. The representatives of each city will contact you and get your measurements, then you can follow the process of sewing your clothes by contacting support.
- You can leave your dress design to us. By providing an overview of the desired dress in form, our designers will design your special dress. After the confirmation stage, they will contact you to get your measurements and you can follow the process of sewing your clothes.
It takes one to two weeks to order each dress.
